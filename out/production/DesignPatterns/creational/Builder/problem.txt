## 🎯 **Problem Statement for Builder Pattern:**

---

### 🚩 **Scenario:**

You are tasked with creating a **Pizza Ordering System** where customers can customize their pizza by selecting:  

✅ **Pizza Size:** Small, Medium, or Large.  
✅ **Crust Type:** Thin Crust, Cheese Burst, or Pan Crust.  
✅ **Toppings:** Multiple options like Onion, Capsicum, Olives, etc.  
✅ **Cheese and Extra Cheese:** Optional Add-ons.  

---

### 🎯 **Task:**
1. Design a `Pizza` class with multiple optional and mandatory attributes.  
2. Use the **Builder Pattern** to allow step-by-step customization of the pizza.  
3. Implement a method `build()` that returns the final `Pizza` object.  

---

### 💡 **Bonus Challenge:**
- Add a method `calculatePrice()` that calculates the price based on selected options.  
- Ensure that the pizza cannot be modified after it is built.
